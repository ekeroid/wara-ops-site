Welcome to the WARA-Ops community Website

The mission of WARA-Ops is to bring together Swedish industry and academia to solve state-of-the-art challenges in data-driven operational research.  We do this through growing an active community of practitioners and researchers with regular events and workshops, and providing a large-scale computational infrastructure on which datasets and models can be stored, annotated, and analysed.
